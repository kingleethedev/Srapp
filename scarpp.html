Problem Statement

Challenges Facing Landlords & Tenants

  • Many landlords struggle with tracking payments and deposits
  • Tenants face delays in receiving payment confirmations
  • Manual systems are error-prone and time-consuming
  • Lack of transparency in rental transactions

Our Solution

SRAPP: A simple, intuitive rental management app

Streamlining Rental Management

  • Efficient tenant management and invoicing
  • Automated payment tracking and reconciliation
  • Automatic SMS confirmations for payments
  • Real-time dashboard with financial overview

Key Features

Tenant Management

Easy tenant creation and contact import for efficient management.

Automated Invoicing

Deposit and recurring invoicing with automated reminders.

SMS Payment Integration

SMS listener for M-PESA, Airtel, and Equitel payments.

Payment Recognition

Automatic payment recognition & acknowledgments.

Statement Generation

Export statements in PDF or CSV format for record keeping.

Dashboard Analytics

Comprehensive dashboard showing balances & due payments.
